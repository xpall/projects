[Chrome Extension] JobInfoClip

I created this Chrome extension to solve the common problem of extracting job data from platforms like OnlineJobs.ph, Indeed, and Upwork. By applying my knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, the extension simplifies the task of gathering and copying job details, offering users a streamlined experience while saving time and effort.

Introduction

     I built this Chrome extension because I despise spamming ctrl+c, alt+tab, and ctrl+v. Inspired by discussions on r/buhaydigital subreddit, it automates data extraction, sparing you from tedious copy-and-paste work and letting you focus on your job search.
