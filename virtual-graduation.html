Virtual Graduation

Drawing on my background in video editing acquired during college, I found a unique application for this skill during the pandemic. Specifically, I played a pivotal role in orchestrating virtual graduation ceremonies for our school spanning three academic years (2020, 2021, and 2022).
This hands-on experience laid the foundation for a comprehensive and engaging project, blending technical expertise with creative flair

     In response to the pandemic's limitations on in-person ceremonies, I spearheaded the creation of an impactful Virtual Graduation experience.
